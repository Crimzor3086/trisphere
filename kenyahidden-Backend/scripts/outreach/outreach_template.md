## Interview Questions

### Company Origin
1. How did the company start? What was the initial opportunity?
2. How many family members have been involved over the years?
3. What was the first major milestone?

### Operational Excellence
1. How would you describe your customer base today?
2. What does distribution look like - geographic coverage?
3. Do you have warehouses, branches, or facilities beyond headquarters?
4. What's your employee count range?

### Growth & Evolution
1. How have you grown over the years - geographically, product-wise, customer-wise?
2. Have you expanded into new markets or products recently?
3. Do you export? If so, where and what's that been like?

### Ecosystem Perspective
1. How do you view the Kenyan business environment?
2. Are you involved in any business associations or chambers?
3. What advice would you give family businesses starting today?

### Why Hidden
1. Have you considered seeking external investment or media attention?
2. What keeps you focused on operations over publicity?
3. How do you think companies like yours contribute to Kenya's economy?
