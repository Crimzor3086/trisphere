# Validation Checklist — KHC-DE

## Validation purpose
Turn high-scoring candidates into **qualified Hidden Champions** using evidence and explicit exclusion checks.

---

## Stage 1: Existence & operations
Check all that apply:
- [ ] Website exists and is relevant to the business
- [ ] Phone number is reachable / matches listings
- [ ] Physical location exists (city/county)
- [ ] Evidence of active operations (recent reviews, recent posts, recent procurement/news)

---

## Stage 2: Longevity evidence
- [ ] “Since/established” visible on website or credible reference
- [ ] Old news/directory entries (prefer 2010–2020 or earlier)
- [ ] Procurement history across years

If longevity evidence is weak:
- [ ] Mark `Needs Validation`

---

## Stage 3: Customer trust signals
- [ ] Google reviews rating and count present
- [ ] Testimonials/case studies present
- [ ] Institutional customer references (NGO/corporate/government)

---

## Stage 4: Exclusion checks (must not confirm without evidence)
VC raised?
- [ ] Evidence of VC funding found → reject
- [ ] No evidence, but unknown → mark `Needs Validation`

Accelerator alumni / startup competitions?
- [ ] Evidence found → reject

Startup ecosystem media presence?
- [ ] Evidence of frequent startup-media features → reject

Heavy LinkedIn presence?
- [ ] Evidence found of startup branding/exec thought-leadership → reduce score / reject depending on strength

Category exclusions:
- [ ] Labeled “AI/fintech/Web3 startup” in a way that indicates startup product positioning → reject

---

## Final decision
- **Validated Hidden Champion**
- **Needs Validation** (insufficient evidence for exclusion visibility or longevity)
- **Rejected** (exclusion confirmed)

Add notes with the evidence links.
