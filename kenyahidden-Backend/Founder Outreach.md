# Founder Outreach — KHC-DE

## Outreach goal
Convert validated candidates into conversations and invitations.

## Outreach pipeline stages
- Discovered
- Researched
- Scored
- Validated
- Profiled
- Contacted
- Interviewed
- Invited to Kuzana

---

## Message framework (initial)
Subject: Invitation to Kuzana — your business journey

Hi [Founder],

We are building Kenya's Hidden Champions, a project dedicated to uncovering exceptional businesses that have quietly built strong, enduring companies outside the startup spotlight.

Your business stood out because of its operational consistency, customer trust, and long-term impact.

We’d love to feature your journey and invite you into the Kuzana network of exceptional builders.

Would you be open to a 20-minute conversation?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Role]
[Phone] | [Email]

---

## Follow-up sequence
- Day 3: short bump referencing one evidence point (e.g., longevity / institutional contracts)
- Day 7: alternate channel (call or WhatsApp) if number exists
- Day 14: final note + ask permission to contact later

---

## Data capture (CRM fields)
- founder name
- email
- phone
- LinkedIn (optional)
- outreach status
- contact date
- response (yes/no/interest)
- next action (schedule, reschedule, close)

---

## Compliance note (practical)
Keep outreach evidence-backed and respectful. Avoid accusations or speculative claims.
